Warwickshire & Beyond

Based at our Alcester workshop, we travel across Warwickshire, North Worcestershire, and the surrounding area to deliver and install bespoke joinery in your home.

Where We Work

Our Service Area

Broom Joinery covers a broad area from our base in Alcester — typically within 25–30 miles of our Arden Road workshop. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, just give us a call on 01789 764 156 — we're always happy to discuss.

Alcester Home Base

Alcester is where we're based, at Arden Road Business Centre, and naturally it's an area we know very well. We've completed numerous joinery projects across Alcester and the surrounding villages — from fitted kitchens in family homes to bespoke staircases and architectural joinery. If you're in Alcester or nearby villages like Kinwarton, Exhall, or Bidford-on-Avon, you're well within our core area.

Stratford-upon-Avon ~8 miles

Stratford-upon-Avon and the surrounding villages — including Shottery, Tiddington, and Welford-on-Avon — are a short drive from our Alcester workshop. We regularly work on bespoke joinery projects for homeowners in Stratford, with a particular demand for fitted kitchens, period-appropriate architectural joinery, and high-quality staircases in the area's many character properties.

Redditch ~10 miles

Redditch and the surrounding areas — including Headless Cross, Webheath, and Astwood Bank — are well within our regular service area. Homeowners in Redditch frequently come to Broom Joinery for fitted bedroom furniture, alcove shelving, and bespoke kitchen cabinetry. We're a popular choice in the area for high-quality work that sets itself apart from standard trade options.

Fitted Furniture Bespoke Kitchens Windows & Doors

Henley-in-Arden ~7 miles

Henley-in-Arden is almost on our doorstep, and we're proud to have completed numerous projects in this beautiful market town and surrounding villages, including Ullenhall, Wootton Wawen, and Claverdon. The area's mix of period cottages and new-build homes creates demand for both traditional joinery restoration and contemporary bespoke pieces — work that suits us perfectly.

Warwick and Royal Leamington Spa — together one of Warwickshire's most affluent areas — are very much within our service zone. The demand for high-quality bespoke joinery in Warwick and Leamington is strong, and we've completed a wide range of projects across both towns, from complete bespoke kitchens to architectural panelling and bespoke staircases in period townhouses.
