Bespoke Joinery Services
in Alcester, Warwickshire

Every piece handcrafted at our workshop on Arden Road — from complete fitted kitchens to individual bespoke furniture commissions.

Service 01

Bespoke Kitchens

A kitchen is the heart of your home, and it deserves to be built properly. At Broom Joinery, we design and handcraft bespoke kitchen cabinetry from scratch — no flatpack components, no compromise on quality.

Whether you favour a classic shaker style, a sleek contemporary design, or something entirely original, we work closely with you to design a kitchen that functions beautifully and looks exceptional for decades to come.

  • Solid timber or veneered cabinet carcasses
  • Handcrafted doors — shaker, slab, beaded, tongue & groove
  • Any paint colour or natural timber finish
  • Islands, larder units, and pantry cupboards
  • Integrated appliance housing
  • Serving Stratford-upon-Avon, Redditch, and all of Warwickshire

Service 02

Fitted Furniture

Fitted furniture makes the very most of your space — and when it's made by hand, it looks infinitely better than anything off the shelf. Broom Joinery designs and builds fitted furniture that becomes a permanent and beautiful feature of your home.

From alcove shelving and reading nooks to home offices, boot rooms, and bedroom wardrobes, we craft each piece to fit your exact space, your aesthetic, and your storage needs.

  • Fitted wardrobes with internal organisation
  • Alcove shelving and cabinetry
  • Home office and study furniture
  • Boot rooms, utility rooms, and laundry storage
  • Under-stair storage
  • Serving Henley-in-Arden, Evesham, Warwick and beyond

Service 03

Staircases

A handcrafted staircase is one of the most dramatic improvements you can make to a home. Whether you want a grand traditional staircase in solid oak, a sleek open-tread floating design, or a renovation of an existing staircase, Broom Joinery has the skill to deliver it.

We work on new-build staircases and complete staircase replacements, always using quality hardwoods and solid joinery techniques that will last a lifetime.

  • Solid oak, ash, and pine staircases
  • Open-tread and closed-riser designs
  • Traditional turned spindles and newel posts
  • Contemporary glazed panels and metal balustrades
  • Staircase refurbishment and repairs
  • Serving Bromsgrove, Worcester, and Warwickshire
Enquire about a staircase →

Service 04

Timber Windows & Doors

There's nothing quite like the quality and warmth of real timber windows and doors. At Broom Joinery, we craft bespoke hardwood windows and doors to your exact specifications — whether you're matching period details in an older property or creating something contemporary for a new build.

All our windows and doors are jointed and finished properly — no PVC substitutes, no shortcuts.

  • Casement and sash windows in hardwood
  • Solid timber entrance doors
  • French doors and bifold doors
  • Garage doors and barn doors
  • Listed building and conservation area work
  • Matching existing period joinery
Enquire about windows or doors →

Service 05

Architectural Joinery

The finishing details are what transform a house into a home. Broom Joinery's architectural joinery work covers everything from wall panelling and dados to coffered ceilings, cornicing, fireplace surrounds, and bespoke built-in cabinetry.

If you're renovating a period property or adding character to a newer home, our craftsmen will design and create architectural joinery that looks as though it has always been there.

  • Wall panelling — shaker, raised & fielded, tongue & groove
  • Fireplace surrounds and overmantels
  • Cornicing, architraves, and skirtings
  • Coffered and beamed ceilings
  • Bespoke cabinetry and dresser units
  • Period restoration and listed building work
