Broom Joinery is a bespoke joinery workshop based in Alcester, Warwickshire. Everything we make is built by hand, made to last, and designed around you.

Our Story

Built on Craft, Honesty, and Pride in the Work

Broom Joinery was founded with a clear purpose: to bring proper, handcrafted joinery back to homes in Alcester and across Warwickshire. We believe that quality joinery isn't a luxury — it's an investment that improves your home and lasts a lifetime.

Our workshop is based at Arden Road Business Centre in Alcester, where every piece we make — from a complete fitted kitchen to a single bespoke door — is designed, crafted, and finished on-site by skilled craftsmen. We don't outsource, we don't cut corners, and we don't send flatpack components dressed up as bespoke work.

What you get from Broom Joinery is genuinely bespoke joinery: designed around your home, built to your exact specification, and fitted with care. That's been our approach since day one, and it will remain so for every job we take on.

What We Stand For

Our Approach to Every Project

🪵

Bespoke, Always

Every piece is designed from scratch for your specific space. We never use standard unit sizes or off-the-shelf components and call it bespoke.

🔨

Traditional Craftsmanship

We use proper joinery techniques — mortise and tenon, dovetails, hand-fitted joints — alongside modern precision machinery where it improves accuracy.

💬

Honest Communication

You'll receive a detailed design drawing and fully itemised quote before any work starts. No hidden costs, no vague estimates, no surprises on completion day.

🏡

Respect for Your Home

Our team arrives prepared, works tidily, and leaves your home clean and undisturbed. We treat every property as if it were our own.

Our Workshop

Made in Alcester — Delivered Across Warwickshire

Our workshop at Arden Road Business Centre, Alcester is where everything begins. It's fully equipped for all aspects of joinery — from raw timber machining through to finishing, painting, and assembly.

We source quality hardwoods and softwoods from reputable UK and European suppliers, choosing materials based on durability, appearance, and suitability for each project. When you visit or call, you'll speak directly to the people who will be building your joinery — not a salesperson.

We serve homeowners and businesses across Alcester, Stratford-upon-Avon, Redditch, Evesham, Warwick, Henley-in-Arden, Bromsgrove, Worcester, and the wider Warwickshire area.
