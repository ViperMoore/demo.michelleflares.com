About AEC Services

20+ Years
Experience Behind
Every Job

AEC Services (Midlands) Ltd was established in 2007 by Richard Lewis, after more than 20 years in the electrical industry. Based in Alcester, Warwickshire, we've built our reputation on honest advice, reliable service and quality workmanship — with the majority of our work coming through recommendations alone.

Being a smaller, owner-run company means we can offer the flexibility, personal service and attention to detail that larger contractors simply can't match. When you call AEC, you speak to the electrician — not a call centre.
