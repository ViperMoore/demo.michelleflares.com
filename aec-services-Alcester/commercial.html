What We Offer

Commercial Electrical Services

We handle electrical projects of all scales for businesses across South Warwickshire and Worcestershire — from small retail units to large industrial premises.

🏢

Office & Retail Electrical Fit-Outs

Moving into new premises or refurbishing your current space? We handle all electrical installation for office and retail fit-outs — from data and power distribution to feature lighting and security systems. We work closely with your project manager to hit deadlines.

  • New build fit-outs
  • Refurbishments
  • Data cabling
  • Power distribution
  • Security systems
⚡

Three-Phase Power Installation

Workshops, manufacturing facilities and large commercial kitchens often require three-phase power for heavy machinery. We design and install three-phase distribution boards and circuits, ensuring your power supply matches your operational demands.

  • Three-phase supply
  • Distribution boards
  • Heavy machinery circuits
  • Commercial kitchens
  • Workshop power
🚨

Emergency Lighting Installation & Testing

Emergency lighting is a legal requirement under BS 5266. We design, install and test emergency lighting systems appropriate for your building type and occupancy — and provide the mandatory annual test certification to keep you compliant.

  • BS 5266 compliant
  • New installations
  • Annual testing & certification
  • Maintained & non-maintained
🔔

Fire Alarm Systems

We install and maintain fire alarm systems to BS 5839 standard. From simple conventional systems to addressable panels for larger buildings, we design a system appropriate for your premises and provide ongoing maintenance contracts.

  • BS 5839 compliant
  • Conventional & addressable
  • New installations
  • Maintenance contracts
💡

Commercial LED Lighting Upgrades

Switching to LED can reduce your lighting energy costs by up to 70%. We survey your current installation, specify the right LED solution, and carry out the full upgrade with minimal downtime. Payback periods are typically under 3 years.

  • Full LED conversion
  • Energy audit
  • Sensor controls
  • DALI systems
  • Up to 70% energy saving
🍺

Public Houses & Hospitality

We have experience working in licensed premises including public houses, restaurants and hotels — understanding the specific requirements of commercial kitchens, cellar refrigeration circuits, external signage and the electrical demands of a busy hospitality environment.

  • Commercial kitchens
  • Bar & cellar wiring
  • External signage
  • Emergency lighting
  • Maintenance contracts
💾

Data Cabling & Network Infrastructure

We install structured data cabling for office and commercial environments — Cat5e, Cat6 and fibre — including patch panels, floor boxes, server room power distribution and cable management. Essential for any new office fit-out or refurbishment.

  • Cat5e / Cat6 cabling
  • Patch panels
  • Floor boxes
  • Server room power
  • Cable management
🔧

Electrical Maintenance Contracts

Reactive and planned maintenance contracts for businesses across Warwickshire and Worcestershire. We keep your electrical installation in safe working order, respond quickly to faults, and carry out periodic inspections and testing to keep you compliant.

  • Planned maintenance
  • Reactive call-out
  • Periodic inspections
  • Fault finding
  • Annual contracts
🔌

Commercial Rewires & Distribution

Ageing commercial wiring is an insurance and compliance risk. We carry out full and partial commercial rewires, upgrade distribution boards and install additional circuits to support business growth — all fully certified.

  • Full commercial rewire
  • Distribution boards
  • Substation work
  • Additional circuits

🔌 PAT Testing — Keep Your Business Compliant

Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) is required under the Electricity at Work Regulations 1989. We carry out on-site PAT testing for all your portable electrical equipment, provide a full test register, and label every item — keeping your insurer and HSE happy.
