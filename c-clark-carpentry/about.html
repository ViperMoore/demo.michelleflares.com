Our story

Alcester-born and bred, built on hard work

Craig Clark has been a carpenter and joiner in Alcester all his working life. After completing his apprenticeship in 2009 and working for several local building firms, he set up C Clark Carpentry to offer homeowners across Warwickshire the kind of care and quality that larger contractors often can't match.

The business has grown steadily through word of mouth — which Craig considers his biggest compliment. Every customer referral is a sign that someone trusted us enough to put their own name behind us. That responsibility keeps standards high on every single job.

Whether it's a set of fitted wardrobes in Studley, a full kitchen installation in Redditch or a staircase renovation in Stratford-upon-Avon, Craig brings the same rigorous attention to detail to every project. No cutting corners, no rushing — just good, honest carpentry done properly.

When he's not on the tools, Craig is a familiar face at Alcester Rugby Club and enjoys spending time with his family in and around the B49 area.

How we got here

The C Clark Carpentry story

From apprentice to trusted local name — a brief look at how the business has grown over the years.

2009

Apprenticeship completed

Craig completed his City & Guilds Level 3 in Site Carpentry and Joinery, training with a respected Warwickshire building firm.

2013

C Clark Carpentry founded

After four years honing his skills with larger contractors, Craig went solo and C Clark Carpentry was born — based in Alcester from day one.

2017

100th kitchen installation

A milestone that confirmed fitted kitchens as a core specialism. Craig invested in specialist tooling and expanded the service offering.

2022

First 5-star Google review streak

13 five-star reviews in a row — all organic, all genuine. A point of real pride for a business built entirely on word-of-mouth.

Today

Warwickshire's trusted local carpenter

Covering Alcester, Redditch, Stratford-upon-Avon and surrounding areas — still the same dedication, the same attention to detail, the same pride in every joint and finish.
