Fitted Kitchens

A fitted kitchen is one of the biggest investments you'll make in your home. We ensure every cabinet, worktop and trim is installed to a standard that will last decades.

  • Full kitchen unit installation (flat-pack or rigid)
  • Worktop cutting, fitting and sealing
  • Appliance integration — hobs, ovens, dishwashers
  • Cornice, pelmet, plinth and end panel fitting
  • Soft-close hinge and drawer adjustment
  • Island units and peninsulas
  • Tiling and splashbacks (in coordination with trades)
