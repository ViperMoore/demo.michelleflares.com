Our Story

A family trade, passed down with pride

M.K. Barkworth & Son has been delivering quality carpentry across Alcester and the surrounding area for decades. What began as a one-man workshop has grown into a trusted family business — where traditional methods meet the highest standards of finish.

Every project, large or small, receives the same personal attention. We work closely with each client to understand their vision, then bring it to life with precision, care, and wood that's chosen to last.

We are based in Alcester, B49 5QT, and cover Warwickshire and the wider West Midlands region.
