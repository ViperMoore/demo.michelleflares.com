Boiler Services

Boiler Repair, Servicing & Installation in Alcester

Your boiler is the heart of your home's heating and hot water. Whether it's losing pressure, making strange noises, or simply refusing to fire up, Peter will diagnose and fix the problem quickly — often same day.

As a Gas Safe Registered engineer, Peter works on all major boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal, and Glow-worm.

  • Boiler fault diagnosis and repair
  • Annual boiler servicing (recommended every 12 months)
  • New boiler supply and installation
  • Combi boiler upgrades
  • Boiler pressure and thermostat issues
  • Flue inspections and gas safety checks

💡 Transparent Pricing

Peter provides a fixed price before starting work wherever possible. Boiler services from £80. New boiler installations from £1,800 supply and fit, depending on boiler and property. Call for a free survey and quote.
