What to Do in a Plumbing Emergency

While you wait for Peter, follow these steps to minimise damage to your home.

1

Turn Off Your Water Supply

Find your stop cock — usually under the kitchen sink or where the water main enters the property. Turn it clockwise to shut off the water. This will stop or slow any flooding.

2

Turn Off Any Electrics Near Water

If water is near electrical outlets, sockets, or fuse boards, turn off the electricity at the consumer unit (fuse box) before touching anything. Safety first.

3

Call Peter on 07870 601505

Call Peter directly — he'll talk you through any immediate steps, confirm he can attend, and give you a realistic ETA. No call centres, no waiting on hold.

4

Contain What You Can

Use towels, buckets, or containers to capture any water and prevent it spreading. Take photos of any damage for your insurance claim.
