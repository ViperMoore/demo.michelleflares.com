About Jay's Gardening Services

A gardener who treats your garden like his own

Hi, I'm Jamie — the face and hands behind Jay's Gardening Services. I'm a family-run business based right here in Ripley, and I cover the whole of Amber Valley and beyond.

Whether your garden's been neglected for years or just needs a regular tidy, I take the same pride in every job. No job is too big or too small — from a quick hedge trim to a full garden overhaul with landscaping and planting.

My customers describe me as honest, hardworking, and reasonably priced. I believe in doing things properly and leaving every garden better than I found it.
